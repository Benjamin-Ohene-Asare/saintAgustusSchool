    Welcome to St.Augustus School Complex

    At St.Augustus School Complex, we provide a centralized and effecient educational environment for student at various levels of basic education, including nursery, primary and Junior High School (JHS). The school aim to enhance educational opportunities, share resources, and create a holistic learnig experience for students,often incorperating facilities and services that support their academic, extracurricular, and community needs

    No silent Followers

    We ensure that our students actively engage in rational questioning, nurturing their critical thinking abilities. Our primary focus lies in fostering holistic personal growth, with the aim of nurturing responsible leaders for the future.

    CONNECTED TO THE WORLD

    Education at our school transcends traditional classroom boundaries. The power of the internet brings us not only the latest information but also captivating, innovative learning methods, enhancing the educational journey for our students.

    360 DEGREE DEVELOPMENT

    Here at our basic school, education is a multi-faceted journey. Our systems and approaches are purposefully designed to foster the holistic development of every child's personality.

    INTELLEGENCE QOUTIENT

    Our progressive curriculum and contemporary teaching techniques empower students to grasp fundamental concepts and stay updated with the latest technological advancements. We establish a strong foundation to pave the way for lifelong success at our basic school.

    About Us

    St.Augustus School is a preparatory school founded in September 2010, open to children of all religions and nationalities. It provides both Ghanaian and International style education with high standards; incorporating the best practices and methods of teaching and learning resources from the best educational systems.
